L'addition


What is it?

L'addition for Android is a very quick and easy-to-use calculator for restaurant bills.
It shows a list of bills and coins, with a plus and a minus button for each of them. Use the buttons to count the money everyone put on the table, and L'addition will display the total and also how much is missing or overpaid.

You can add custom amounts (for people paying by card), and there is also a special line with a configurable amount for "meal tickets".
Supported currencies: US dollar, pound sterling and euro.

Requirements

  • A device running Android 1.0 or more recent

License

L'addition and its sources are in the public domain.
