WorldTour


What is it?

WorldTour for Android periodically sets your home wallpaper to a live view of the beautiful Eiffel Tower, Paris and other locations from all around the world!
The image is fetched every 30 min. from a live webcam so you'll always enjoy the current view of the selected location.

This app is now recommended by T-Mobile US, and is featured on their AppPack.

Requirements

  • A device running Android 1.0 or more recent
