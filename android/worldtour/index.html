WorldTour

Hi!
This is the page for WorldTour, the Android app that displays beautiful webcams from all over the world on your wallpaper.

WorldTour periodically sets your home wallpaper to a live view of the beautiful Eiffel Tower, Paris and other locations from all around the world!
The image is fetched every 30 min. from a live webcam so you'll always enjoy the current view of the selected location.
The app is free (ad-supported) and is recommended by T-Mobile.
